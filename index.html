About OrderSaap

OrderSaap started as a chill side project built by Rashdan Hamzah .

I wanted to keep my food orders always within reach — because WhatsApp is where I already spend most of my time. I’m always hungry T_T and I seriously hate apps and signups. Hopefully, I built OrderSaap so I can order food directly from my WhatsApp chat.

Cheers ^_^
